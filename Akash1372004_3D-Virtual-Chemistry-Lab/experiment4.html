Experiment 4 — Action of Heat

In this experiment, you will perform dry heating tests on unknown mixtures to observe solid colour changes (hot vs. cold) and check the evolved gases with chemical test papers to identify thermally active ions.

⚠️ Safety Protocol: Test tubes will become extremely hot. Use a test tube holder and wear virtual safety goggles!

Unknown Mixtures
Procedure
1. Take a small amount of mixture in a dry test tube
2. Heat carefully over a Bunsen burner flame
3. Record solid colour changes (hot vs cold) and evolved gas
4. Run confirmatory tests on the evolved fumes
5. Identify the active thermal ions present
Warning: Wear safety goggles. High heat can cause glass stress.
