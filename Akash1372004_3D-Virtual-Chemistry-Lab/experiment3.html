Experiment 3 — Carbonate Test

In this experiment, you will perform qualitative analysis of unknown salts using dilute Hydrochloric Acid (HCl) and check the evolved carbon dioxide (CO₂) gas using limewater to confirm the presence of Carbonate (CO₃²⁻).

⚠️ Safety Protocol: Dilute HCl is an irritant. Handle with care and wear virtual safety goggles!
